" I was feeling pretty low one day, and decided to try my luck on Tinder. I wasn't really expecting much, but I figured it couldn't hurt to put myself out there. To my surprise, she messaged first and beat me to it, right away we started chatting. We quickly discovered that we had a lot in common and we both shared the same sense of humor. We made plans to meet up in person mid covid crisis , yes i know naughty haha but to our suprise 1 and half months down the track we finaly got to meet in person.

We've been together for over 2 years now and every day just keeps getting better. I never would have thought that I could find love on a dating app, but Elysha proved me wrong. I'm so glad I swiped right that day on tinder, it was the best decision I've made."
