Transport

Please ensure that you pre arrange your transport, we recommend carpooling where possible. The location of the Estate is not a good place to get taxis or Ubers at short notice, therefore, we ask that you make sure you have a plan on how you are getting to and from the Estate. If you are planning on drinking any amount of alcohol, we kindly ask that you do not drive and carpool with a sober driver.

Getting to Matakana estate


Our wedding will be held at Matakana Estate at 568 Matakana Road, MATAKANA, Warkworth 0985, Please refer to the map of the Estate below, When you arrive you will drive up a long gravel driveway, you will see signs for The Lodge and The Cellar Door, Please see on the map below where you can park, The Ceremony and drinks and mingling after the Ceremony will be held at The Lodge, and the Reception will be held at Cellar Door, it is about a 10 minute walk between The Lodge and the Cellar Door, Please ensure that you allow enough time to park so that you are at The Lodge at 2:30pm.





Things to do in Matakana


If you're planning to spend a weekend or longer in Matakana, there is an abundance of exciting activities and places to explore in the area. We have compiled list of recommended things to do and must-visit locations:

1. Visit the Matakana Village Farmers' Market: Indulge in a vibrant atmosphere while exploring the local produce, artisanal food, and crafts. (Bride and Groom’s Favourite) https://www.matakanavillage.co.nz/market

2. Immerse yourself in art and culture at Brick Bay Sculpture Trail: Wander through the captivating outdoor sculpture trail surrounded by native bush, vineyards, and farmland. https://www.sculptureum.nz/

3. Explore Goat Island Marine Reserve: Put on your snorkelling gear and dive into the crystal-clear waters of this marine reserve, known for its diverse marine life and stunning underwater scenery. https://www.doc.govt.nz/parks-and-recreation/places-to-go/auckland/places/cape-rodney-okakari-point-marine-reserve-goat-island/?tab-id=50578#4

4. Delight your taste buds and enjoy the kid friendly activities at Matakana Country Park: Indulge in a variety of gourmet treats, including cheese, and chocolate while enjoying the picturesque countryside. https://www.matakanacountrypark.co.nz/

5. Discover Matakana Coast Wine Country: Embark on a wine tasting tour and savour the region's exceptional wines produced in picturesque vineyards.https://matakanawine.co.nz/

6. Take a leisurely stroll along Omaha Beach: Enjoy the pristine white sand, breathe in the fresh ocean air, and soak up the beauty of one of the region's most scenic beaches. https://omahabeach.co.nz/

7. Catch a movie at the iconic Matakana Cinemas: Watch the latest releases in a charming boutique cinema, complete with cozy seating and a relaxed ambiance. https://www.matakanacinemas.co.nz/now-playing

8. Experience the charm of Morris & James Pottery: Witness the skilled craftsmanship of locally made pottery and explore the delightful gallery showcasing unique ceramic pieces. https://www.morrisandjames.co.nz/

9. Enjoy a leisurely bike ride along the Matakana River: Rent a bicycle and pedal along the scenic river trail, soaking in the peaceful surroundings and lush greenery. https://www.helloauckland.co.nz/city-info/get-around/bikes-&-motorbikes/matakana-bicycle-hire

10. Dine out at one of the many highly rated local restaurants:


Plume Café and Plume Restaurant (Around the corner from the wedding venue) - www.plumerestaurant.co.nz

Charlie Gelato Garden (Around the corner from the wedding venue) - www.charliesgelato.co.nz

Matakana Market Kitchen - www.matakanamarketkitchen.co.nz

The Stable Restaurant - www.stablesmatakana.co.nz

Matakana Village Pub - www.thematakana.co.nz/

Brick Bay Vineyard - www.brickbay.co.nz

Chocolate Brown - www.chocolatebrown.co.nz
