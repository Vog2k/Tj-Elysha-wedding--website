Matakana Estate

To our friends and family: We're so excited to celebrate our wedding with you. Find all the details you need to know about our big day here.

Matakana Estate situated in the heart of Matakana, a hidden gem of a wine region, less than an hour’s drive north of Auckland. Matakana Coast is a must-visit destination for a holiday in New Zealand, with fine wines, local foods, busy markets and stunning beaches.

Matakana Estate is the largest winery in the region, spanning 35 hectares of land. Our boutique-style wines are available to taste at our cellar door, a luxury lodge can be booked for holiday stays and vacations.
