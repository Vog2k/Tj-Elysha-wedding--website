The Venue

Matakana Estate

Matakana Estate is situated in the heart of Matakana, a hidden gem of a wine region, less than an hour’s drive north of central Auckland. Matakana Coast is a must-visit destination for a holiday in New Zealand, with fine wines, local foods, busy markets and stunning beaches.

Matakana Estate is the largest winery in the region, spanning 35 hectares of land.
